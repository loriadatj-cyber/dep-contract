# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Welcome questions and different levels of experience.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing private package or registry information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sustained disruption, and
publishing another person's private information are not accepted.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Maintainers may
remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions
that violate this policy, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors
for serious or repeated violations.
